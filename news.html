Ritual Effect to join P.O.D. in Riga and Tallinn in June 2021

Expand

ALPHA OMEGA Management has a huge pleasure to announce that Dublin based alternative rockers RITUAL EFFECT will be joining the US alternative metal heroes P.O.D., as special guests, next June in Riga and Tallinn, as follows:

June 21st 2021; Godvil Concert Hall, Riga

June 22nd 2021; Helitehas Klubi, Tallinn

We are absolutely ecstatic to be opening for P.O.D. Their albums have had a huge influence on our music. We know they have a loyal and dedicated fan base and we can't wait to play for them!
